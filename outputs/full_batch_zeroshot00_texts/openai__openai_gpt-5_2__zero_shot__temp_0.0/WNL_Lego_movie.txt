Lego made much more profit in the first half of 2014. This was helped by The Lego Movie, which was very popular in the US and UK.

The Danish toy company sold more toys in Europe, the Americas, and Asia. Many children bought Lego sets linked to the movie. The movie came out in February. By the first weekend in April, it had made more than $250m in the US and £31m in the UK.

The movie cost about $60m to make. People said it was almost perfect marketing, because it was fun to watch and also made people want to buy Lego products.

Lego’s finance director, John Goodwin, said the movie toys helped results in the first half of 2014. He said they would see what happens after the movie is released on DVD in the second half of 2014.

Lego’s operating profit for the first six months rose by 12% to $630m. Sales rose by 11% and were more than three times higher than six years earlier.

Lego’s chief executive, Jørgen Vig Knudstorp, said the result was very good, even though the economy was tough. He said it came from Lego’s ability to design, launch, and deliver products that children around the world really want.

Lego is based in Billund. It began making plastic bricks in 1949. By the 1970s, it was a common children’s toy around the world. But the company had big problems and was close to failing in 2003. Knudstorp became chief executive, ending 70 years of family control. He removed hundreds of extra products so the company could focus again on its famous bricks.

In April, the company opened its first factory in China and opened an office in Shanghai to lead growth in the world’s second-biggest economy.
