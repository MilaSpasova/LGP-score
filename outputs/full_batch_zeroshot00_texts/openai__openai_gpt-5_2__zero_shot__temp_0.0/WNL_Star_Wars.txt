A long time ago, people went to the movies and saw Star Wars. It showed a galaxy far away. Big ships fought in space. There were droids and Wookiees. A ship called the Millennium Falcon could make the Kessel Run in less than 12 parsecs.

That was in 1977. In 2015, Star Wars was still very popular. There was news about a new movie about Han Solo. There were also rumors that Darth Vader might appear again.

Paul Dergarabedian, a media analyst at Rentrak, said many fans around the world keep watching for new posters, new trailers, and other news. He said it is hard to think of another movie series that makes people feel so excited.

The newest big wave of Star Wars excitement started after Disney bought Lucasfilm from George Lucas in 2012. Disney paid $4 billion. Disney also said there would be three more episodes: VII, VIII, and IX. They also planned spin-off movies.

Now there are details about the second spin-off movie. It will tell the early story of Han Solo. Harrison Ford played Han Solo in the first three movies. The Han Solo movie is planned for May 2018.

Before that, Episode VII will come out in December 2015. JJ Abrams directed it. It is called Star Wars: The Force Awakens. Episode VIII is planned for 2017. Another spin-off movie, Rogue One, is planned for 2016.

Rogue One will tell about a rebel mission to steal the plans for the Death Star. This is important in the first movie from 1977. People were also very interested in rumors that Rogue One might include Darth Vader. This showed how strong Star Wars memories are for fans.

Disney and Lucasfilm are making many movies in one Star Wars “universe,” with many stories and characters. They are doing something like Marvel Studios, which Disney bought in 2009.

Disney also uses strong marketing. Dergarabedian said it was a great idea to put all six older Star Wars movies on streaming services. He said this helps build excitement for the new movie and helps fans think about Star Wars again.

Some people think Star Wars does not need help to stay popular. For at least the last 20 years, fans have followed the “Expanded Universe.” This includes many novels, comic books, video games, and merchandise.

This extra material started in 1978. It grew much more in the 1990s as the internet became bigger. Fans still enjoy role-play and events. In the UK, Secret Cinema has made money by putting on a live show of The Empire Strikes Back.

Michael Rosser, news editor for Screen International, said this shared universe and strong nostalgia keep Star Wars at the top. He said the original movies made a huge world of characters and ideas.

Rosser said that for years people have wondered how the different story parts would connect. He said the new movie, because it goes back to characters like Han Solo and Luke Skywalker, looks like it will connect to the original Star Wars better than the prequels did.

Rosser meant the three movies George Lucas made from 1999 to 2005: The Phantom Menace, Attack of the Clones, and Revenge of the Sith. These movies told the story of Luke Skywalker’s father, Anakin, who becomes Darth Vader. The reviews were not very strong, but the prequels still made $2.5 billion worldwide. Before that, “special editions” of the first three movies made $469 million in 1997.

Rosser said this shows the power of Star Wars. Even when people were disappointed, the movies still made a lot of money.

The article asks if there is a risk that movie studios will only care about brands and not about movies as art.

Rosser said he does not think so. He said they want the Star Wars series to last a long time and to keep the quality high. He also said they want people to go to movie theaters, when many people stay home for entertainment. He said you do not want to watch Star Wars on an iPhone, so he does not think it will lose power soon.

Dergarabedian expects The Force Awakens to make a lot of money when it comes out in December 2015. He said it could have a record opening for December and could make at least $1 billion worldwide. He said Star Wars is the biggest movie brand.
