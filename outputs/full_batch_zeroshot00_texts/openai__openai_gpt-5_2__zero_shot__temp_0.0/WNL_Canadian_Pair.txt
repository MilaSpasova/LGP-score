A Canadian man named Jordan Axani became famous after he offered a free trip around the world to a woman with the same name as his ex-girlfriend. He has now returned to Canada from the trip with Elizabeth Quinn Gallagher. People hoped they would fall in love, but they did not. Axani said they became like brother and sister.

Axani, who is 28 and from Toronto, made news in 2014. He had booked a three-week vacation with his ex-girlfriend, but they broke up. He could not change the name on the plane tickets, so he offered the ticket to any Canadian named Elizabeth Gallagher.

Elizabeth Gallagher, a 23-year-old student from Cole Harbour, Nova Scotia, answered his Reddit post. She goes by the name Quinn. Axani chose her from other people with the same name. Before the trip, Quinn said she had a “pretty serious” boyfriend.

After they got back to Toronto, Axani said the trip was never meant to be romantic. He said he did not see Quinn in a romantic way and there was no romantic future for them. He said she was a good friend, like a little sister, and she felt the same.

Axani said it took time for them to feel comfortable traveling together. It took about a week to understand each other. At first, they had to learn the dos and don’ts of traveling together. Later, they had a good rhythm. They joked together and also knew when the other person needed space.

They visited Milan, Venice, Vienna, Prague, Khao Lak (in Thailand), and Hong Kong. Axani said the trip was “fantastic.” He said Prague was a favorite place. In about two and a half days there, they met about two dozen people.

Axani said many people followed them on Twitter and Instagram. In Hong Kong, people even recognized them on the street. He said it was a real adventure and that they learned a lot about themselves and each other.

Axani returned to Toronto at 3am on Monday, 12 January. The trip ended quickly, and he went straight to a board meeting for his charity, A Ticket Forward. He started the non-profit after his Reddit post became very popular. He wants to offer round-the-world trips to survivors of abuse, cancer, and war.

Axani also said he is talking with companies about turning the story into a television show or film, but he did not say what it would be. He said many production companies were interested. He also said he was not looking for his next Elizabeth Gallagher yet. He said, “life happens and we’ll see.”
