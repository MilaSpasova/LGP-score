In 1977, people watched Star Wars in cinemas. They saw battles in space. They saw droids and Wookiees. They saw a spaceship called the Millennium Falcon. It could do the Kessel Run in less than 12 parsecs.

Now it is 2015. Star Wars is close to its seventh movie in cinemas. Interest is still very strong. There is news about a new movie about Han Solo. There is also talk that Darth Vader may appear again.

Paul Dergarabedian, a media analyst at Rentrak, said fans all over the world watch closely for new posters, new trailers, and other news. He said it is hard to think of another movie series that creates so much passion and excitement.

The newest wave of Star Wars excitement started in 2012. Disney bought Lucasfilm from George Lucas for $4bn. Disney announced three more episodes: VII, VIII, and IX. Disney also planned spin-off movies.

Now Disney has shared details about the second spin-off. It is an origins story about Han Solo. In the first three films, Harrison Ford played Han Solo. This new movie is planned for May 2018.

Episode VII will come out in December 2015. JJ Abrams directed it. It is called Star Wars: The Force Awakens. Episode VIII is planned for 2017. Another spin-off, Rogue One, is planned for 2016.

Rogue One will tell a rebel mission to steal the plans of the Death Star. This is important in the first film from 1977. People are also very interested in a rumour that Rogue One will include Darth Vader. This shows the power of Star Wars nostalgia.

Disney and Lucasfilm want to build a bigger Star Wars movie universe. They are using a similar idea to Marvel Studios films. Disney also bought Marvel in 2009.

Disney has also used strong marketing. Dergarabedian said it was a great idea to put all six existing Star Wars films on streaming services. He said this helps build excitement for the new film.

Star Wars has had strong support for at least the last two decades. This can be seen in the “Expanded Universe”. It includes many novels, comic books, video games, and merchandise.

This extra material started as early as 1978. It grew a lot with the internet in the 1990s. Fans still join in and role-play. In the UK, Secret Cinema has had success with a live event of The Empire Strikes Back.

Michael Rosser, news editor for Screen International, said this shared universe of nostalgia helps keep Star Wars a top film series. He said the original films made a huge universe of characters and possibilities.

Rosser said people have wondered for years how the different story lines would connect. He said the new film, because it goes back to Han Solo and Luke Skywalker, seems to connect to the original Star Wars more than the prequels did.

Rosser was talking about three films George Lucas directed from 1999 to 2005: The Phantom Menace, Attack of the Clones, and Revenge of the Sith. These films tell the story of Anakin, Luke Skywalker’s father, who becomes Darth Vader. Many people did not give them great reviews. But they still made $2.5bn worldwide. In 1997, special editions of the first trilogy made $469m.

Rosser said this shows the power of Star Wars. Even when fans were disappointed, the films still made a lot of money.

Some people worry that movie studios may only care about brands and not about cinema itself. Rosser said he does not think so. He said they want the series to last a long time and keep the quality high. He also said they want people to go to cinemas, because many people stay home for entertainment. He said you do not want to watch Star Wars on your iPhone, so he does not think it will lose popularity soon.

Dergarabedian also expects very big business for The Force Awakens in December 2015. He said it could have a record opening for December. He said it should make at least a billion dollars worldwide. He said Star Wars is the ultimate movie brand.
