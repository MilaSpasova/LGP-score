The top-selling book on Amazon in the US is not Harper Lee’s new novel Go Set a Watchman. It is not George RR Martin’s Song of Ice and Fire series. It is not Zoella’s Girl Online. Instead, Scottish illustrator Johanna Basford is selling the most books with her adult colouring books. Her books are at the top of Amazon.com’s bestseller lists.

Basford’s book Secret Garden has very detailed drawings of plants and animals. It has sold 1.4 million copies worldwide so far. Her new book Enchanted Forest has already sold just under 226,000 copies. Famous people also like these books. Zooey Deschanel shared a link on Facebook. The South Korean pop star Kim Ki-Bum shared a picture on Instagram for his 1.6 million followers.

Eleanor Blatherwick, who leads sales and marketing at the publisher Laurence King, said the last few weeks have been “crazy” and “madness”, but also great. She said they knew the books were beautiful, but they did not expect such big success.

In the UK, other adult colouring books are also selling well. Richard Merritt’s Art Therapy Colouring Book is fourth on Amazon’s list. Millie Marotta’s Animal Kingdom is seventh. A mindfulness colouring book is ninth. Basford’s books are second and eighth. That means 5 of Amazon.co.uk’s top 10 books are adult colouring books.

The UK publisher Michael O’Mara has sold almost 340,000 adult colouring books so far. Ana McLaughlin said the trend is strong because people now see colouring as a way to relax. Their first book was in 2012, Creative Colouring for Grown-Ups. It sold well. In 2014, Art Therapy made the trend much bigger. She said selling the “anti-stress” idea helped adults feel it was OK to do something they might think is for children.

The Mindfulness Colouring Book also says colouring can help people feel calm. It tells readers to take a few minutes each day and “colour your way to peace and calm”.

Basford said it is relaxing to do something offline, or “analogue”. She said it is creative, but a blank page can feel scary. With a colouring book, you only need to add colour. She also said it reminds people of the past. Some people told her they used to colour secretly when their kids were asleep. Now it is accepted, and these books are for adults. She said the art in her books is very detailed.

Basford lives in Aberdeenshire. She was surprised by the reaction after Secret Garden came out in 2013. She said she had been asked to make a children’s book, but she wanted to make one for grown-ups. It was not a trend then. The publisher agreed. She thought only people like her would want it. She only wanted to make a book she would like. She was surprised to see the category grow.

Now she is working on a third book. Michael O’Mara already has 17 adult colouring books for sale, and will increase this to 22 by May. New titles will include The Classic Comic Colouring Book and The Typography Colouring Book. McLaughlin said the trend is huge and is not slowing down. She said buyers like to show that they can stay inside the lines.

McLaughlin said people post their pictures on Twitter and Instagram. People feel proud because the drawings are so detailed. She said you do not need to be an artist, but each picture is different. People send the publisher photos of their work, framed and laminated. She said demand is very large, and she thinks people may take their kids’ pictures off the fridge and put up their own instead.
