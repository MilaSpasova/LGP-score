Lego made much more profit in the first half of 2014. This was helped by the Lego Movie, which was very popular in the US and the UK.

The Danish toy company sold more in Europe, the Americas, and Asia. Children bought many products linked to the film. The film came out in February. By the first weekend in April, it had made more than $250m in the US and £31m in the UK.

The movie cost about $60m to make. People said it was almost perfect marketing, because it was entertaining and it made people want to buy Lego products.

Lego’s finance director, John Goodwin, said the Lego Movie products helped results in the first half of 2014. He said it was not yet clear what will happen after the movie comes out on DVD in the second half of 2014.

Lego’s operating profit for the first six months went up 12% to $630m. Sales rose 11%, to more than three times the amount from six years earlier.

Lego’s chief executive, Jørgen Vig Knudstorp, said the result was very good. He said Lego has grown a lot in recent years, even though the economy has been tough. He said the company can develop, launch, and distribute Lego products that children around the world really want.

Lego is based in Billund. It started making plastic bricks in 1949. By the 1970s, Lego was a common children’s toy around the world. But in 2003, the company almost collapsed. Knudstorp became chief executive and ended 70 years of family rule. He stopped making hundreds of extra products and focused again on the main Lego bricks.

In April, the company opened its first factory in China. It also opened an office in Shanghai to help it expand in the world’s second-biggest economy.
