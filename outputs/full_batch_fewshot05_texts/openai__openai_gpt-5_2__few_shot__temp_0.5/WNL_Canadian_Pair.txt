A Canadian man named Jordan Axani became famous after he offered a free trip around the world to a woman with the same name as his ex-girlfriend. He went on the trip with Elizabeth Quinn Gallagher, but they did not fall in love. Axani said they became like brother and sister.

Axani made news in 2014. He had booked a three-week vacation with his ex-girlfriend, but they broke up. He could not change the name on the plane tickets. So he offered the ticket to any Canadian named Elizabeth Gallagher.

A 23-year-old student from Cole Harbour, Nova Scotia, replied to his Reddit post. Her name is Elizabeth Gallagher, and she uses the name Quinn. Axani chose her. Before the trip, Quinn said she had a “pretty serious” boyfriend. Even so, some people still hoped the two travellers would fall in love. But they did not.

After they returned to Toronto, Axani said the trip was never romantic. He said it was only friendship. He said Quinn was a good friend and like a little sister. He said she felt the same way.

Axani said it took time to get used to travelling together. It took about a week to understand each other. They had to learn the rules of travelling together. Later, they could joke together and also know when the other person needed space.

They visited Milan, Venice, Vienna, Prague, Khao Lak in Thailand, and Hong Kong. Axani said the trip was “fantastic”. He said Prague was a favourite place. Many people there reached out to them. In two and a half days, they met about two dozen people.

Axani said many people followed them on Twitter and Instagram. In Hong Kong, people even recognized them in the street. He said it was a real adventure and they learned a lot.

Axani returned to Toronto at 3am on Monday, 12 January. The holiday ended quickly. He went straight to a board meeting for his charity, A Ticket Forward. He started the non-profit after his Reddit post became very popular. He wants to offer round-the-world trips to survivors of abuse, cancer, and war.

Axani also said he is talking about turning the story into a TV show or a film, but he did not say what it will be. He said many production companies are interested. He also said he is not looking for a new Elizabeth Gallagher right now. He said, “life happens and we’ll see.”
